## 🏛️ Corporate Identity & Strategic Framework

### The Architecture of Possibility
The Nigerian real estate landscape in 2026 is defined by a paradox of immense opportunity shadowed by a systemic crisis of confidence. As the national housing deficit expands and macroeconomic volatility persists, the market in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), Abuja, has shifted from a volume-led scramble to a value-led maturation. In this high-stakes environment, **ONE SQUARE METER (1SQM)** is positioned not merely as a property developer but as a transformative force designed to redefine the semiotics of luxury and the ethics of delivery.

### ⏸️ The Art of Pressing Pause (2022 - 2024)
*“Imagine pausing a symphony mid-performance, not because the musicians faltered, but to let the audience hear the silence before the crescendo.”*

Post-2022, One Square Meter by Dantata made a bold strategic move: we pressed pause on sales and slowed down construction. This was not a retreat, but a rebellion against inflation and compromised quality.

*   **The Why:** Amidst post-COVID chaos, election drama, and skyrocketing building costs, we refused to let market volatility dictate the quality of our homes. While competitors sold out inventories at fixed prices only to abandon projects later, we held unsold units close.
*   **The Result:** Our buildings outlived the lies. Investigations and audits found project assets far exceeding liabilities. We absorbed inflationary blows so our subscribers wouldn't have to.
*   **The Return:** Today, with settlements reached and arbitration resolving, we are back. Construction has resumed, handovers are ongoing, and new sales are activated.

### Brand Philosophy: The Semiotics of the "Square Meter"
The choice of the name **ONE SQUARE METER** is a radical act of transparency in a market often obscured by grandiosity. It signals a shift from the macro-promises of "estates" to the micro-perfection of the "square meter".

**The ONE SQUARE METER Creed:**
> "We exist to transform lives by building more than spaces—we build possibilities. Rooted in courage and guided by vision, we champion the bold dreamers, the builders of legacies, the believers in better. Where others see limits, we see potential. Where others hesitate, we move with purpose."

## 🚀 Quick Start & Technical Setup

### Prerequisites
- Node.js (v16+)
- npm or yarn

### Installation
```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```
Visit: **http://localhost:5173**

### Tech Stack
- **Framework**: React 19 + TypeScript
- **Routing**: React Router DOM
- **Build Tool**: Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide React
