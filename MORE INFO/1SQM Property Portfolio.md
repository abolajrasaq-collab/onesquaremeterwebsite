## About 1SQM by Dantata

1SQM by Dantata is a fractional real estate platform operated by the **Dantata & Sawoe Consortium**, one of Nigeria's most storied construction and development groups. The platform democratises access to premium Abuja real estate by allowing investors to own high-quality residential and commercial units at multiple price points — from student co-living hostels to premium detached duplexes and commercial arcades.

Every property in the 1SQM portfolio is designed and delivered to the highest construction standards, drawing on decades of expertise in Nigerian real estate. Units are available at various build stages — **DPC, Shell, Advanced Shell, or Fully Furnished** — giving investors flexibility to enter at the most financially strategic point.

The company's investment thesis is straightforward: Abuja is a high-growth, supply-constrained market with strong formal-sector demand. Pairing that fundamentally robust market with purpose-built design, professional management, and transparent pricing creates assets that deliver both reliable rental income and above-market capital appreciation.

### Why Student Accommodation in Jabi?

Student accommodation consistently outperforms conventional residential rentals in Nigeria's capital. The academic calendar creates reliable, recurring demand — beds are let before the previous tenants vacate. The Jabi corridor is unique: it is the only part of Abuja where two internationally recognised universities (Nile and Baze) create an overlapping demand zone, effectively doubling the catchment relative to a single-university location.

Premium student demand also means tenants pay higher rents for quality, reducing price sensitivity and enabling management to maintain premium specifications with minimal vacancy.
